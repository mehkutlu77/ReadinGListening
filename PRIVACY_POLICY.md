# Privacy Policy for LinguiVance

**Last Updated:** March 10, 2026

## 1. Introduction

LinguiVance ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web-based platform designed to help you learn English through interactive lessons, flashcards, readings, and listening exercises.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information:** When you create a profile, we collect information such as your name, email address, and language preferences.
- **Learning Data:** We collect information about your learning progress, including vocabulary mastered, quiz scores, reading completion, and listening exercise performance.
- **Profile Photo:** Optional profile photos you upload are stored locally on your device and in your account (if synced).
- **Contact Information:** If you contact us with feedback, complaints, or inquiries, we collect the messages and contact details you provide.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, and browser type used to access the application.
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the application (pages visited, features used, time spent on activities, etc.).
- **Session Data:** Timestamps of your learning sessions and activity logs.
- **Technical Data:** IP address (for analytics), error logs, and crash reports to improve the application.

### 2.3 Cookies and Local Storage
We use browser storage technologies (localStorage, sessionStorage) to:
- Save your learning progress and preferences locally on your device
- Remember your language settings and theme preferences (light/dark mode)
- Store notification preferences and reminder schedules
- Track spaced repetition scheduling data for optimal learning intervals

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Learning Experience:** To deliver personalized English learning content based on your proficiency level and learning history.
2. **Progress Tracking:** To monitor your learning progress, calculate statistics, and provide weekly summaries and achievement badges.
3. **Notification Services:** To send you optional daily reminders and achievement notifications to encourage consistent learning.
4. **Service Improvement:** To analyze usage patterns and improve the application's features, user interface, and content.
5. **Technical Support:** To diagnose technical issues, fix bugs, and provide customer support.
6. **Communication:** To respond to your inquiries, feedback, and support requests.
7. **Research:** With your consent, to conduct educational research on language learning effectiveness (anonymized data only).

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- Most of your learning data (vocabulary progress, quiz scores, reading history) is stored **locally on your device** using browser storage technologies.
- This data is not automatically transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly enable cloud synchronization.
- Your data remains under your control and is not sold or shared with third parties.

### 4.2 Security Measures
- We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data storage for sensitive information.
- Access to admin features requires authentication credentials.
- We use HTTPS encryption for any data transmitted between your device and our servers.
- We regularly review our security practices to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information.

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Learning progress data is retained as long as your account is active.
- You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.
- We retain aggregated, anonymized statistics indefinitely to improve our service.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

### 5.2 Limited Sharing
Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:
- **Service Providers:** We may share data with third-party vendors who provide technical infrastructure, analytics, or customer support services, strictly under confidentiality agreements.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, court order, or government request.
- **Authorized Research:** With your explicit consent, we may share anonymized learning data with educational researchers to improve language learning methods.
- **Business Transfers:** If LinguiVance is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

### 5.3 Third-Party Links
Our application may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 Access and Portability
You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request a copy of your data in a structured, portable format
- Understand what data we collect and how we use it

### 6.2 Correction and Deletion
You have the right to:
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete information in your profile
- Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
- Opt-out of optional features like notifications and tracking

### 6.3 Exercising Your Rights
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the information provided in Section 10.

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 7.1 Browser Storage Technologies
We use localStorage and sessionStorage to enhance your experience:
- **localStorage:** Persists your learning preferences, theme settings, and progress data across browser sessions
- **sessionStorage:** Stores temporary data needed during your current session only

### 7.2 Analytics
We use anonymized analytics to understand how users interact with our application. This helps us improve features and user experience. Analytics data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

### 7.3 Disabling Cookies
You can control browser storage through your browser settings:
- Clear localStorage and cookies at any time
- Disable storage through privacy or security settings
- Note: Disabling storage may affect the application's functionality

## 8. Children's Privacy

LinguiVance is designed for language learners of all ages. We comply with applicable children's privacy laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) in the United States.

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.
- If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13 without proper consent, we will delete it promptly.
- Parents/guardians may contact us to review, update, or request deletion of their child's information.

We implement safeguards consistent with this Privacy Policy.
